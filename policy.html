LVLD Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 June 2026

LVLD respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the LVLD app.

LVLD is a simple finance budgeting app designed to help you track spending, organise expenses, create budgets, and better understand your money habits.

LVLD is operated from Australia and is available to users globally. We handle personal information in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Information We Collect

LVLD is built to collect only the information needed to provide its budgeting and finance tracking features.

Budget and Expense Information

  • Information you choose to enter, such as expenses, income, budgets, categories, notes, and savings goals.
  • Transaction details you manually add, including amounts, dates, merchants, descriptions, and categories.
  • This information is saved so you can view your history, track progress, and manage your budget over time.

iCloud Sync

  • LVLD uses Apple iCloud to store and sync your app data where iCloud is enabled on your device.
  • This allows your budgets, expenses, categories, notes, and settings to remain available across your Apple devices.
  • iCloud is provided by Apple and is connected to your Apple ID. Apple handles iCloud data in accordance with its own privacy and security practices.
  • LVLD does not sell or share your iCloud data with advertisers.

App Identifiers

  • The app may use basic identifiers needed to support app functionality, syncing, subscriptions, and restoring access.
  • We do not use these identifiers to track you across other apps or websites.

Subscription and Payment Information

  • If LVLD offers paid features or subscriptions, payments are handled by Apple through the App Store.
  • We do not receive or store your full payment card details.
  • We may receive basic subscription status information from Apple so we can unlock paid features.

Usage and Diagnostics Data

  • LVLD may collect basic usage information, such as feature interactions, to improve app performance and usability.
  • LVLD may collect crash reports and performance diagnostics through Apple’s built-in tools.
  • We do not use this information to build an advertising profile of you.

Information We Do Not Collect

LVLD does not collect:

  • Advertising identifiers for ad targeting
  • Contacts from your device
  • Location data
  • Bank login details
  • Payment card numbers
  • Data for tracking you across other apps or websites

How We Use Information

Information handled by LVLD is used only to:

  • Provide budgeting, expense tracking, savings, and finance organisation features
  • Save your budgets, transactions, categories, notes, and settings
  • Show charts, summaries, trends, and spending insights
  • Sync your data using iCloud where enabled
  • Manage subscription access
  • Improve app stability, performance, and usability
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal information to advertisers or third parties. We do not share your personal information with advertisers, and we do not use your data for cross-app tracking.

Data Storage, Sync, and Retention

  • Your budgets, expenses, categories, notes, and settings are retained so LVLD can function and your history remains available to you.
  • Where iCloud is enabled, your LVLD data may be stored and synced through your Apple iCloud account.
  • You can delete budget and expense information from within LVLD where supported.
  • LVLD does not create a separate LVLD account. If you want to remove your synced app data, you can delete your LVLD data from within the app where supported or manage app data through your Apple ID/iCloud settings.
  • Usage and diagnostic data may be retained in aggregated or de-identified form to help us improve the app.

Third-Party Services

LVLD may use third-party services solely to operate and improve the app.

Apple Services

  • iCloud for storing and syncing app data across your Apple devices where enabled.
  • StoreKit for In-App Purchases and subscriptions where paid features are offered.
  • Apple’s crash reporting and performance diagnostics tools.

No third-party services are used for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access. LVLD relies on Apple iCloud for data storage and sync where enabled, and Apple provides security protections for iCloud services.

No system is perfectly secure, so we encourage you to keep your device secure, keep your Apple ID protected, and use strong authentication where available.

Your Privacy Choices

You can:

  • View, edit, or delete budget and expense information within LVLD where supported
  • Manage iCloud access through your Apple ID and iCloud settings
  • Manage app permissions through iOS Settings
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we hold about you
  • Manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID settings

To make a privacy request, contact us using the details below. We will respond within a reasonable period.

Children’s Privacy

LVLD is not directed to or intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us so we can address the issue.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in LVLD, our practices, or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
